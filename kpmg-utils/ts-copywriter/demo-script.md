# TS Copywriter Demo Script

**Duration:** 2-3 minutes
**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover

---

## Opening (15 seconds)

**[Show ChatGPT interface with TS Copywriter GPT selected]**

> "TS Copywriter is a writing assistant built specifically for financial due diligence professionals. It takes rough notes or poorly written financial text and transforms it into polished, Big 4-quality analysis—enforcing active voice, data anchoring, and precise terminology. Let me show you how it works."

---

## Demo 1: Rewrite Mode (45-60 seconds)

**What to show:** The tool's core capability—fixing common mistakes in FDD writing

**Copy/paste this into ChatGPT:**

```
Rewrite:

We analyzed the Company's revenue streams and found that there was significant customer concentration. The top 5 customers accounted for a large portion of total revenue. We believe this appears to be a risk that should be considered by the buyer. Our procedures ensured that this analysis fairly represents the business.
```

**What to say while it processes:**

> "Here I've pasted some typical first-draft FDD text. Notice it uses passive voice, vague words like 'significant' and 'large portion,' and includes assurance language like 'ensures' and 'fairly represents'—all things that violate professional standards."

**After the response appears:**

> "Look at the transformation. The rewrite uses active voice, replaces vague language with specific numbers, and removes the assurance terms entirely. It also cites the source of the data and presents findings before implications."

**Expected output will show:**
- "Significant" → actual percentage
- "We analyzed" → removed
- "We believe" → removed
- "ensures" → removed
- Active voice throughout

---

## Demo 2: Draft Mode (45-60 seconds)

**What to show:** Creating polished prose from rough notes

**Copy/paste this into ChatGPT:**

```
Draft:

Section: Nonrecurring Expenses
- Company had $450K one-time legal settlement in Q3 2024
- Related to employee lawsuit from 2022
- Management confirmed won't happen again
- Need to add back to EBITDA
- Also $125K consulting fees for ERP implementation
- ERP project finished in June 2024
```

**What to say while it processes:**

> "Now let's try Draft mode. I'm giving it messy bullet points—the kind of notes you'd jot down after a management call. Watch how it creates publication-ready prose."

**After the response appears:**

> "From six bullet points, we now have a polished paragraph that's ready to go into a client deliverable. It anchors every claim to specific numbers, uses proper financial terminology, and follows the standard structure for nonrecurring adjustments."

---

## Demo 3: Polish Mode (30-45 seconds)

**What to show:** Quick cleanup of decent text

**Copy/paste this into ChatGPT:**

```
Polish:

Target's gross margin declined from 42% in FY23 to 38% in FY24. The decline was primarily driven by increased raw material costs, which rose by approximately $1.8M year-over-year. In addition, the Company experienced higher freight costs due to supply chain disruptions in Q2 and Q3. Management indicated that they have renegotiated supplier contracts effective January 2025, which they expect will restore margins to historical levels.
```

**What to say:**

> "Polish mode is for text that's already pretty good but needs tightening. This paragraph is decent, but watch how the tool sharpens it—removing hedging language like 'approximately' and 'indicated that,' and making the cause-and-effect relationships clearer."

---

## Closing (15 seconds)

> "That's TS Copywriter—it enforces the writing standards that Big 4 partners expect, so your team can focus on the analysis instead of wordsmithing. Whether you're rewriting, drafting from scratch, or polishing existing work, it delivers consistent, professional output every time."

---

## Tips for Recording

1. **Pause briefly** after pasting each prompt to let viewers read it
2. **Highlight key changes** in the output (you can point with your cursor)
3. **Keep energy up** — this is a productivity tool, so enthusiasm sells
4. **If time is tight**, cut Demo 3 (Polish mode) and expand on Demos 1 & 2
