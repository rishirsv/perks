# Business Overview

- Business overview
1561710 Alberta Ltd., d/b/a Pure Canadian Gaming Corporation (i.e. the “Company”), is a privately-held Canadian gaming operator, acquired by ONCAP in FY11. Headquartered in Edmonton, AB, the Company operates four casinos in three different cities (i.e. Edmonton (2), Calgary (1) and Lethbridge (1)).
The Alberta Gaming and Liquor Commission (“AGLC”) regulates and controls all the gaming activities in the province of Alberta.
As of September 30, 2018, the Company operates approximately 2,875 slot terminals, 107 VLTs, 92 table games, 39 poker tables and 4 entertainment venues supported by several bars and restaurants across all four properties. The Company has 22.5% share of provincial slot cash played and 29.8% share of provincial table game drop.
The Company’s primary revenue streams comprise of: slots (39%), table games (31%), food & beverage (15%), poker (6%) and other (9%) in TTM September 30, 2018.
The Company recently opened a new poker room in Casino Calgary in May 2018 and an entertainment room in Casino Yellowhead in December 2017.
Information overview and access
The analysis contained in this report is prepared based on the following sources of information and access:
Data room access: Key information provided by Management in an electronic data room including monthly Management discussion & analysis reports, monthly trial balances by property and other financial records; and
Management meetings: Several discussions held with the Company’s Management, including George Goldhoff (CEO), Anna Borissova (CFO) and Chenelle Beck (Controller).
