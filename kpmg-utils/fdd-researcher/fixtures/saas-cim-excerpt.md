# Acme Software — CIM Excerpt

## Company Overview

Acme Software provides workflow management software to small and mid-sized field service businesses, including HVAC contractors, plumbers, and electricians.

## Business Model

- **Pricing:** Per-seat SaaS model at $49-99/user/month
- **Contracts:** Annual subscriptions with monthly billing option
- **Add-ons:** Usage-based dispatching module ($0.50/dispatch)

## Key Metrics (FY24)

## Customer Base

- 85% US-based customers
- Target segment: 10-100 employee field service companies
- Low customer concentration (top 10 = 8% of ARR)

## Technology

- Cloud-native, multi-tenant SaaS platform
- AWS-hosted infrastructure
- Mobile-first design (iOS/Android apps)
