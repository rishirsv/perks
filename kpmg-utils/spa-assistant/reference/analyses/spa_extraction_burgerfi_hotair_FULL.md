## Part 22: Suggested Revisions

### Revision 1: Overdraft / Negative Cash Routing

**Current Language:** No explicit overdraft/negative balance routing in Cash or Indebtedness definitions.

**Section Reference:** Cash (PDF p. 11/108); Indebtedness (PDF p. 17/108).

**Issue:** Overdrafts can fall through definitions or be double-counted, directly impacting Net Indebtedness and purchase price.

**Suggested Revision:** Add explicit routing: overdrafts/negative balances treated as Indebtedness.

**Rationale:** Eliminates a high-frequency completion account dispute category.

**Likely Pushback:** “No overdrafts exist.”

**Fallback Position:** Add representation that no accounts are overdrawn at closing cut-off and require bank statements supporting.

### Revision 2: ASC 842 Lease Liability Treatment

**Current Language:** Indebtedness includes GAAP capital leases; operating leases not explicit.

**Issue:** Lease liabilities can be material and drive valuation.

**Suggested Revision:** Explicitly include or exclude ASC 842 operating lease liabilities (tie to model intent).

**Fallback Position:** Include only above a threshold or only for specified leases.

### Revision 3: Extend Leakage Claim Period

**Current Language (verbatim):**

```text
(c) There shall be no reduction in the BFI Consideration Share Amount under Section 2.05(b) unless Buyer has notified Seller in writing of
its breach of Section 2.05(a), or the claim under Section 2.05(b), stating in reasonable detail the nature of the breach and, if practicable, the
amount claimed, within sixty (60) days of the Closing Date.
```

**Issue:** 60 days may be insufficient to discover leakage, especially with missing schedules/exhibits.

**Suggested Revision:** Extend to 180–365 days, with thresholding or audit-rights compromise.

**Fallback Position:** Keep 60 days for immaterial claims, extend for claims above $X or discovered via audit.

### Revision 4: Clarify Transaction Expenses Scope (Bonuses, Payroll Taxes, D&O Premium)

**Current Language:** Transaction Expenses broad but not explicit on employee payouts and D&O premium.

**Suggested Revision:** Explicitly include transaction/retention bonuses, severance, related employer payroll taxes, and D&O premium required by Section 5.16, treated as Leakage.

**Fallback Position:** Include only those listed on a schedule with caps.
