### 2.Working Capital / Net Working Capital

**Definition Name:** Working Capital / Net Working Capital

**Section Reference:** Exhibit A (Definitions) + Exhibit B, PDF pp. 65–66; 79–81

**Full Verbatim Text:**

> “Closing Working Capital” means, as of the Effective Time, (i) the current assets of the Company minus (ii) the current liabilities of the Company as calculated in accordance with the Calculation Principles.
> “Closing Date Working Capital Adjustment” means an amount, if any, which may be a positive or negative number, by which the Closing Working Capital (a) is less than negative $13,000,000 or (b) is greater than negative $11,000,000; then in either case, only such surplus or deficit, as the case may be, shall be the Closing Date Working Capital Adjustment.
> “Estimated Closing Date Working Capital Adjustment” means the Seller’s good faith calculation of the Closing Date Working Capital Adjustment.

**Plain-English Summary:**

Working capital is measured at the Effective Time as current assets minus current liabilities, calculated under Exhibit B. The adjustment is collared: only deviations outside -$13.0m to -$11.0m trigger an adjustment, and only the amount outside the band is adjusted.
