# Counsel Question Bank

> Consolidated from the “Counsel Questions to Raise” sections of the analyses. Questions are grouped into recurring themes; each question lists the deals that raised it.

## Purchase Price / Consideration
1. In fixed-price public mergers, could paying agent/withholding mechanics delay payment to holders, and should interest/cash-in-lieu/fractional share mechanics be clarified? *(Raised in: CyrusOne/Cavalry (Merger), Deutsche Telekom/AT&T (T‑Mobile USA), MoneyGram/Mobius (Merger), iRobot/Amazon (Merger))*
2. Are there any circumstances where the **payment timing** could be materially delayed (beyond standard transmittal mechanics), and if so, should interest apply? (Cross-check Section 4.2 timing and any regulatory holdbacks — none apparent.) *(Raised in: iRobot/Amazon (Merger))*
3. Are there any dividend/split adjustment mechanics that could effectively change the $90.50 per share (e.g., Section 2.07 adjustments) and are those consistent with board communications? (Section 2.07, PDF p.11 / A-7). *(Raised in: CyrusOne/Cavalry (Merger))*
4. Clarify “funded and outstanding” scope for Closing Indebtedness—does it include overdrafts/cash management facilities and capital lease liabilities only, or broader? *(Raised in: Newgistics/Logistics Management)*
5. Confirm **exact day-count mechanics** for Additional Consideration (e.g., whether Closing Date = Sept 18, 2023 yields 1 day). *(Raised in: Aerojet Rocketdyne/L3Harris (Merger))*
6. Confirm explicitly (in closing checklist / disclosure) that only the “Special Pre-Closing Dividend” reduces the $24.25, and that permitted regular dividends are not intended to adjust consideration (to avoid later “double dip” arguments). *(Raised in: Bluerock Residential/Badger (Merger))*
7. Confirm how Additional Consideration applies to **equity awards cash-outs** (it appears included via “Per Share Merger Consideration” in award conversion provisions). *(Raised in: Aerojet Rocketdyne/L3Harris (Merger))*
8. Confirm that “Cancelled Shares” includes treasury shares and shares held by Parent/Merger Sub (and clarify treatment of any DRS/book-entry oddities) to ensure the cash funding amount is correct. *(Raised in: Bluerock Residential/Badger (Merger))*
9. Confirm whether Additional Consideration is payable for **all outstanding shares** and whether any **cap** exists (none stated in definition). *(Raised in: Aerojet Rocketdyne/L3Harris (Merger))*
10. Confirm whether any class of shares, subsidiary-held shares, or equity equivalents are treated differently and whether any elections could impact REIT/partnership structure (Section 2.01(b), PDF p.7 / A-3). *(Raised in: CyrusOne/Cavalry (Merger))*
11. Confirm whether “Average Trading Price” vs “Average Adjusted Closing Price” is used consistently (cash election uses Average Trading Price). *(Raised in: Deutsche Telekom/AT&T (T‑Mobile USA))*
12. Does “Working Capital Overage/Underage” in the **Purchase Price** definition intend to be calculated using **Closing Net Working Capital** (not Estimated)? If yes, revise definitions to eliminate ambiguity. *(Raised in: TreeHouse (Meal Prep))*
13. Is the intent that the Company be delivered “debt-free” and effectively with no pre-close liabilities? If yes, should that be stated in the price clause vs. relying on covenants/exhibits (see 6.15 + Excluded/Included Liabilities)? *(Raised in: Durata/Pfizer)*

## Cash Definition
1. Are there interim operating covenants restricting extraordinary cash leakage (dividends, unusual distributions) instead of a cash adjustment? (See conduct covenants in Section 5.01, PDF pp.35–40 / A-31 to A-36). *(Raised in: CyrusOne/Cavalry (Merger))*
2. Confirm how cash pooling / sweep arrangements are treated under the Applicable Accounting Principles (especially if balances net across entities). *(Raised in: TreeHouse (Meal Prep))*
3. Confirm methodology for “fees or taxes” that would become payable: what is included, who calculates, and whether any discounted value is allowed instead of full exclusion. *(Raised in: HealthEquity/Luum)*
4. Confirm that “cash at close” is economically embedded in Net Capital / balance sheet mechanics and not separately adjusted. *(Raised in: National Financial Services/Fiserv)*
5. Confirm whether any separate covenant requires the Company to maintain **minimum cash** or limit new debt pre-close (these are typically handled through operating covenants, not purchase price true-up). *(Raised in: Aerojet Rocketdyne/L3Harris (Merger))*
6. Confirm whether any “marketable securities” are illiquid or subject to restrictions that should make them “Restricted Cash” instead. *(Raised in: TreeHouse (Meal Prep))*
7. Confirm whether restricted cash (non-freight) is included. *(Raised in: Newgistics/Logistics Management)*
8. Define precisely the “freight payment cash account” and how “reserved amounts” are determined at Closing Time. *(Raised in: Newgistics/Logistics Management)*
9. Do interim covenants (Section 5.1, PDF pp. 41–46) adequately restrict extraordinary cash leakage (dividends, unusual distributions, affiliate payments)? *(Raised in: MoneyGram/Mobius (Merger))*
10. If the Company holds cash, is Seller required to sweep it out pre-closing as part of Excluded Assets transfer? *(Raised in: Durata/Pfizer)*
11. If the client is expecting an EV bridge, confirm they understand this agreement is **equity-value per share with no adjustment** (risk is allocated via interim covenants and MAE/closing conditions, not completion accounts). *(Raised in: iRobot/Amazon (Merger))*
12. If the economic intent was “cash-free/debt-free,” where is that captured? (Answer: it is not; consideration is fixed.) *(Raised in: CyrusOne/Cavalry (Merger))*

## Indebtedness Definition

3. Any letters of credit outstanding (amounts and whether “liability … in respect of” is properly quantified)? *(Raised in: Newgistics/Logistics Management)*
4. Are there any intercompany notes/payables to Seller or affiliates that should be settled or treated as debt? *(Raised in: Newgistics/Logistics Management)*
5. Are there any other debt instruments not captured by Section 5.17 that must be refinanced/paid for closing to occur (e.g., change-of-control triggers in other contracts)? *(Raised in: MoneyGram/Mobius (Merger))*
6. Confirm whether any covenants restrict new borrowings or require payoff of specific debt at closing (often critical where price isn’t adjusted). *(Raised in: Aerojet Rocketdyne/L3Harris (Merger))*
7. Confirm whether “litigation… including legal fees” is intended to cover only accrued, booked liabilities on the Closing Balance Sheet, or also unasserted/contingent matters. *(Raised in: HealthEquity/Luum)*
8. Do the debt documents require minimum notice periods that could extend beyond the expected Closing timetable, and are those baked into the “Marketing Period” / End Date planning? (Sections 1.2, 5.17; PDF pp. 9–10, 62). *(Raised in: MoneyGram/Mobius (Merger))*
9. Does the targets’ treasury setup include overdrafts, sweeps, or cash pooling? If yes, where are they captured? *(Raised in: Newgistics/Logistics Management)*
10. Does the “keep well” restriction unintentionally restrict routine credit support required by customers/landlords (e.g., performance bonds) beyond the permitted exceptions? (Section 5.01(b)(ii)(A)(2), PDF p.36 / A-32). *(Raised in: CyrusOne/Cavalry (Merger))*
11. Is “intercompany debt” relevant given the Company is a former Pfizer subsidiary (and if so, is it addressed via Excluded Liabilities)? *(Raised in: Durata/Pfizer)*
12. What are the specific capex budget numbers and the “Capital Expenditure Funding Framework” (Company Disclosure Letter) that govern the permitted additional Indebtedness basket? (Section 5.01(b)(ii)(A)(6), PDF p.36 / A-32). *(Raised in: CyrusOne/Cavalry (Merger))*
