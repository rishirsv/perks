Quantification
In the table below a high-level quantification of the potential impact of ASC 606 for FY17 and FY18 is included. The quantification is intended to visualize the impact of allocating fees for activities completed in the Think/Deploy phase that are related to set-up activities to the performance obligations in the contract. This allocation results in a deferral of revenues compared to current accounting.
In the quantification 3 scenarios are included
:
Scenario 1 visualizes the impact of ASC 606 in the case that no set-up activities are identified in the Think/Deploy phase. In this case, revenue recognized is expected to be similar to the current accounting and therefore the
quantification
reflects no impact on revenue recognized.
In scenario 2 and 3, the estimated impact is calculated in the case that 20% and 40% respectively of the total fees for the Think/Deploy phase relate to set-up activities. As shown in the table, this leads to a deferral of revenues, which will be recognized from FY19 onwards.
