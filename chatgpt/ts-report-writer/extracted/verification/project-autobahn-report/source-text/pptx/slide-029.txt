Other considerations
Velicept site payments:
The Company accrues payments related to site visit costs based on number of visits of the end of the month per system output. Invoices are received subsequently from various sites for the actual costs, which Management represents may arrive 1-3 months later, and may differ from the costs accrued. As VEL2001 and VEL2002  projects wind down, there may be a mismatching of billings to Velicept and costs invoiced from the sites. You should ensure that you are not responsible for any site payments to be made, for which Axiom has received the revenue. As of Dec-18, the accrued balance is $2.4 million.
