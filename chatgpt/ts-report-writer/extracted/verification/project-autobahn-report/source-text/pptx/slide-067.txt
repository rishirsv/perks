Appendix 5
Direct Costs
The following items within direct costs are of note:
CRO:
These costs are related to external CRO costs paid to CRMG. There has been an increase in these costs over the historical period primarily due to the start-up of the Velicept projects, specifically VEL2002 in Feb-18 and VEL2001 in May-18. In FY17, CRO costs comprised the entirety of total direct costs.
Patient Cons:
These costs relate to the patient costs paid to sites. This has grown from FY18 primarily due to the increased number of studies undertaken by the Company.
Investigator:
These costs are related to expenses paid to CRT for organizing investigator meetings. These costs include flights, hotels, meals, etc. There were 150 investigators for four days.
Lab costs:
These costs relate to specific contracts that pertain to managed services agreements for Velicept.
Source: Management provided information, KPMG analysis
