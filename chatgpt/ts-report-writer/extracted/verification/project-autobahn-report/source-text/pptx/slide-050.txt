ASC 606 – Impact assessment
Step 1 – Identify the contract
Narrative
Current
accounting
Revenue contracts are accounted for once persuasive evidence that
an arrangement exists.
Requirements of ASC 606
The assessment of
whether a contract exists is based on whether enforceable rights and obligations exist. Furthermore, ASC 606 has additional requirements before revenue can be recognized, such as a probability assessment for the collection of the consideration and an assessment of the commercial substance.
Implications of adoption
Although
ASC 606 has additional requirements related to identifying the contract, this is not likely to result in a change compared to current practice.
