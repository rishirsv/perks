Narrative
Key judgement areas
In discussion with management they indicated a view that the various promises in the ‘Think/Deploy’ phase all provide the customer incremental benefit to that which they will receive from simply granting access to the hosted application and therefore are not set-up or administrative activities. This is because each study is a unique process that requires significant customization and configuration of the application to make it ready for use by the customer.
However, in our experience, adoption of the new requirements typically requires a company to perform a detailed analysis of the various activities in the implementation phase, grouping them into either implementation or set-up activities.
To the extent there are activities that relate to set-up activities, the fees related to these activities will need to be allocated to the performance obligations in the contract, which will result in a portion of them being deferred to the ‘Launch’ phase.
