SR&ED income removal (decrease of $0.6 million):
Represents an adjustment to remove all SR&ED income. The Company’s status as a CCPC (Canadian Controlled Private Company) has changed as the Company is now ineligible to receive cash refunds for 35% of eligible expenditures. They are now eligible to receive tax credits for future taxes payable for 15% of eligible expenditures, as opposed to cash refunds. Due to the nature of the change in the way the credits will be received, we have proposed an adjustment to remove all SR&ED income.
