Private jet NWC adjustment:
It is our understanding that Axiom pays for all private jet costs and then subsequently allocates out the proportionate cost used by the other entities (
Thinkworks
and J2ASM) out of their P&L. It is our understanding that this arrangement will cease upon closing as each entity will pay for their own costs up-front. As such, the proposed adjustment  estimates the payables impact of the non-Axiom costs, and removes them from the historical period.
