Financial   ■ The Company’s finance and accounting team is comprised of two assistant controllers and a cost analyst, with several                                                    n/a
reporting     accounting clerks supporting bookkeeping, accounts payable, accounts receivable, and payroll processing.
■ The Company’s finance department historically included an experienced controller. Management has not replaced the
controller upon his retirement in April 2019.
■ Historically, the Company has hired Rehmann for year-end tax reporting, compilation and review engagements for
FY16 and FY17, compilation and (first time) financial statement audit for FY18.
■ The Company has utilized Rehmann for year-end closing procedures. These include accrual entries that are only
performed at year-end (e.g. allowance for doubtful accounts, accrued vacation, etc.) and intercompany eliminations,
which are performed at year-end on a summary basis.
■ It is our understanding that Bank reconciliations were not performed during the historical period.
■ The Company has a targeted monthly financial close by the 15th with internal financials prepared by the 20th of each
month.
■ The Company uses Encore and Viewpoint accounting systems. Encore supports billing, scale operations, routing,
dispatching, paperless routes, online payments and route performance for the solid waste operations. Viewpoint,
(formerly known as Spectrum) is the primary ERP software which supports all accounting processes. Management
does not track pricing and volume information with exception to Traverse City MRF.
■ We have noted reconciling differences throughout the financial information provided, as detailed in the supporting
analysis and Appendices to this report. We note the following other key limitations in information/scope as at the date
of this report, including:
– Entity elimination entries on a monthly basis for the historical period, at the detailed trial balance level. We have
received elimination entries as at 31-Dec-17 and 31-Dec-18 at a summary/grouped account level;
– Reclassification entries on a monthly basis for the historical period. We have received reclassification entries as at
31-Dec-17 and 31-Dec-18;
– We have received details of related party balances within net working capital accounts for the historical period, but
cannot confirm we have been provided all related party balances on a monthly basis for each entity;
– 2019 forecast/budget, as the Company does not prepare annual budgets/forecasts;
– Explanations for variances between the entity P&Ls and the operating division P&Ls. Management represented the
variances are due to adjustments not factored into in the operating division P&Ls.
