# Business Overview

- The company’s primary offering includes general dentistry, hygiene, and specialty services such as orthodontics, endodontics, oral surgery, periodontics, and prosthodontics.
- The company has a presence in every major Canadian urban center; approximately 79% of the Canadian population lives within roughly a 20km radius of a practice.
- As of March 2025, trailing twelve-month adjusted EBITDA was $254 million.
- The Company’s selection criteria include:
-   - Minimum acquisition thresholds: at least $2.0 million in revenue and at least $400,000 in EBITDA.
-   - Multiple dentists and hygienists.
-   - Strong clinical reputation and standards.
-   - Attractive location and facility type.
- Transaction perimeter includes independent owner-practitioner acquisition opportunities that create additional white space for growth.
- The Company’s strategy includes a proprietary patient portal and PRM platform, strategic partnership with VideaHealth, and support functions through internal systems such as Oracle NetSuite and Workday.
