# Eventscape — Confidential Information Memorandum

## Executive Summary

Eventscape is a leading event management software platform headquartered in Toronto, Ontario. The Company provides end-to-end event planning, registration, and analytics solutions to corporate event teams across North America.

## Company Overview

Founded in 2018, Eventscape has grown to become a trusted platform for mid-market and enterprise organizations managing corporate events, conferences, and hybrid experiences. The platform combines registration management, attendee engagement tools, and post-event analytics in a single cloud-based solution.

**Headquarters:** Toronto, Ontario, Canada
**Employees:** 127 FTEs
**Founded:** 2018

## Business Model

Eventscape operates a subscription SaaS model with tiered pricing:

| Tier | Monthly Price | Included Events | Target Segment |
|------|---------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Professional | $899/month | Up to 10 events | Mid-market |
| Business | $2,499/month | Up to 50 events | Enterprise |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Large enterprise |

Additional revenue streams:
- **Payment processing:** 2.5% + $0.50 per registration transaction
- **Professional services:** Implementation, training, custom integrations ($175/hour)
- **Add-on modules:** Mobile app builder, virtual event studio, API access

Notable customers include: Shopify, Manulife, KPMG Canada, Telus, and SickKids Foundation.

## Technology

- Cloud-native platform built on AWS (Toronto and Virginia regions)
- Modern tech stack: React frontend, Node.js backend, PostgreSQL database
- SOC 2 Type II certified; PIPEDA and GDPR compliant
- 99.95% uptime SLA
- Open API with 40+ pre-built integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, Slack)

## Growth Strategy

1. **US Expansion:** Opened Boston sales office in Q3 2024; US now 38% of new bookings
2. **Product-led growth:** Launched self-serve tier in January 2025
3. **Virtual/hybrid events:** Added virtual event studio post-COVID; now 15% of platform usage
4. **Channel partnerships:** Signed reseller agreements with 3 event agencies

## Competition

Eventscape competes with Cvent (enterprise), Bizzabo (mid-market), Hopin (virtual), and legacy tools like Eventbrite and RegFox. The Company differentiates through:
- Superior analytics and ROI reporting
- Hybrid event capabilities
- Canadian data residency option
- White-label customization
