# Custom GPT SPA Assistant

Custom GPT SPA Assistant is a reusable M&A analysis workspace for Share Purchase Agreement diligence. It is designed around structured playbooks that extract financial terms, assess economics, benchmark against market norms, and prepare negotiation guidance from one uploaded SPA.

## What this project contains

- `docs/` — internal product documentation, requirements, playbooks, and research.
- `reference/` — source agreements, extracted analyses, playbook templates, prompts, and validation/annotation datasets.
- `dist/` — built artifact exports from the assistant.

## Core capabilities

- 8 playbooks covering financial definitions, purchase price mechanics, working-capital/net-debt analysis, earnouts, reps and warranties, commercial terms, redline review, and negotiation roleplay.
- SPA extraction and negotiation workflows designed for finance-focused due diligence.
- Evidence-first output format with bias-aware summaries and explicit gap/missing-item handling.
