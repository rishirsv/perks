I'm building a Custom GPT that analyzes Share Purchase Agreements (SPAs) from a **financial due diligence perspective**. The goal is to create the best SPA analyst in the world.

The assistant will have 8 "playbooks"—structured, repeatable workflows that run when triggered. Each playbook needs:
1. **What to look for** (the checklist)
2. **How to analyze it** (the framework)
3. **What output format** (structure, columns)
4. **Common pitfalls** (what to avoid)
5. **Market benchmarks** (what's normal vs unusual)

I need your expertise to inform these playbooks. Please answer each research prompt below thoroughly.
