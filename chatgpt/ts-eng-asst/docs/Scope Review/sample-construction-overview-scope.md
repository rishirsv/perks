# Sample Scope Output: Construction (Overview Sections Only)

This sample reflects what gets assembled for `industry = construction` for overview sections.

## Business Overview

1. Meet with Target's officers and management in order to develop an understanding of operations, including its:
   1. Basis of financial information;
   2. Organization structure;
   3. Historical growth by geography; and
   4. Finance function, financial reporting framework and internal control environment.

## Accounting Overview

1. Read Target's financial statements and discuss Target's accounting policies and practices with management, including:
   1. Finance function, financial reporting framework, and management reporting relationships (including processes, timing, structure of financial reporting, and interaction with the information/accounting systems);
   2. Significant accounting policies and recent or contemplated changes in accounting principles, procedures, or estimates;
   3. Significant accounting estimates (e.g. bad debt provision, rebates, capitalized R&D, etc.); and
   4. Intercompany accounts and related party transactions, if any.
2. Consider if the following accounting policies have been applied consistently within and across historical periods:
   1. Cash versus accrual accounting policies;
   2. Capitalization policies;
   3. Depreciation policies; and
   4. Differences between interim and year-end procedures; and
   5. Revenue recognition policies;
